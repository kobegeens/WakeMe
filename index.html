WakeMe

Hey!

This is the site for my app called WakeMe. Both the site and app are still in development.

What?

WakeMe is an open-source alarm clock app for Android with privacy in mind.

Why?

I needed a project to participate in the Neighboord program by Hack Club, and this seemed like something that could be made fairly simple, but with enough time could also grow into a fully fledged app with lots of functions.
