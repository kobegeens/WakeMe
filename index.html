WakeMe

Hey!

This is the site for my app called WakeMe. Both the site and app are still in development.

What?

WakeMe is an open-source alarm clock app for Android with privacy in mind.

Why?

I needed a project to participate in the Neighboord program by Hack Club.

This seemed like something with a low barrier to entry, but also something that, with enough time, could grow into a fully fledged app with a wide range of features.
